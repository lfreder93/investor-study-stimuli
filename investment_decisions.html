Investment Decision
As a reminder, you and your family have received a $10,000 gift from relatives and have decided to invest this money. As the designated decision-maker, you have two options for how to proceed.
Option 1
Self-Invest
You manage the investment yourself, deciding how to allocate the $10,000 across a combination of investment types of your choosing. You take full responsibility for the allocation and its ongoing management.
Option 2
Delegate to a Financial Advisor
You hand the decision to a professional financial advisor, who will allocate the $10,000 on your behalf based on their expertise. The advisor charges a standard fee of 1.5% for their services.
Which option would you like to choose?

Please note that if you choose to self-invest, you will not need to pick specific investments as part of this study.
