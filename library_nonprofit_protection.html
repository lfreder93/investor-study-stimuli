InvestorShield Learning Resource Center

Know the risks before you invest

Welcome to the InvestorShield Learning Resource Center. Our goal is to help you recognize the risks and red flags that every investor needs to watch out for. As an independent, nonprofit organization with no financial stake in your investments, we’ve designed this series of short articles as a starting point — investing involves a lot of moving parts, and these concepts are just the beginning. After completing them, you’ll know exactly what to look for — and what to look out for — before putting your money on the line. ⚠️⚠️⚠️
